# Government Pollution Guidelines (India)

## PM2.5 Standards
- **Good**: 0-30
- **Satisfactory**: 30-60
- **Moderately Polluted**: 60-90
- **Poor**: 90-120
- **Very Poor**: 120-250
- **Severe**: >250

## Action Plan for High Pollution
1. **Moderate**: Sensitive groups should reduce outdoor exertion.
2. **Poor**: Everyone should reduce prolonged outdoor exertion.
3. **Severe**: Avoid all physical activity outdoors. Schools may be closed.

## CO2 Standards
- **Normal**: 350-450 ppm
- **Elevated**: 450-1000 ppm (Poor ventilation)
- **High**: >1000 ppm (Drowsiness, poor air quality)
